Types of novels

Adventure fiction:
Adventure fiction is a type of fiction that usually presents danger, or gives the reader a sense of excitement.

Detective fiction:
While not exclusively, many mysteries in these novels are murders or crimes of some sort. Detective fiction is a popular kind of mystery novel.

Science fiction:
Sci-fi novels are speculative stories with imagined elements that don't exist in the real world.

Hestorical fiction:
They are stories that are set in the past and based on historical events
